About Me

Hi, I’m Angelo C. Lacson, a full‑stack web developer based in the Philippines. I'm currently working as a Senior Software Developer at AMCS Group Inc., where I help design and maintain enterprise-level software solutions.

Previously, I worked at iRely Inc. as a Full Stack Developer, and before that, I held the role of IT Officer at Robinsons Retail Holdings Inc.. I've also gained valuable experience from organizations like Accenture, Cloudstaff, and Naxos Global Distribution.

My work focuses on building scalable, maintainable, and user-friendly systems using modern technologies and clean architecture principles. I’m also passionate about process automation and continuous delivery, having undergone DevOps training under Amazon.

In addition to my full-time role, I also take on freelance development projects. I currently work with an Australian company, Human Fequency, where I deliver custom-built web solutions tailored to client requirements.

I’m also passionate about building my own tools and apps. One of my ongoing personal projects is Spenalyze, a financial tracking app designed to help users better understand and manage their spending through intuitive visualizations and insights.

Whether I’m building for enterprise clients or creating tools for everyday users, I’m focused on writing clean code, solving real-world problems, and delivering seamless web experiences.
